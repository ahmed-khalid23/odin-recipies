Steak with Mash Potato

Description

The following is a recipie of a steak dinner for one with mash potato that should take you 30 minutes, providing you with a tasty and nutritious recipie.

Ingredients

  • Rib-eye Steak
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • Thyme
  • Rosemary
  • Kerrygold butter
  • 2 medium potatoes
  • Milk

Steps

Mash Potatoes

  1. Boil water
  2. Wash potatoes
  3. Peel potatoes with a peeler
  4. Place potatoes in pot and cover with water and place on max heat
  5. Wait for around 20 minutes
  6. Check if cooked by poking with fork and seeing if it passes through easily
  7. Turn of heat and drain water
  8. Mash fully with a masher
  9. Add large chunk of butter and half cup of milk
  10. Place on medium heat and whisk thoroughly with hand whisk until smooth and creamy

Steak

  1. Put steak on chopping board and rub salt and pepper on both sides
  2. Put large amount of olive oil and knob of buter in pan and put on high heat
  3. Add steak
  4. Add whole garlic cloves, thyme and rosemary
  5. Baste steak constantly with spoon as it cooks
  6. Check underside of steak to see if it has developed a crust after a few minutes, if it has: flip
  7. Again, baste steak as it cooks
  8. Remove once other side has also developed crust

Serving

  1. Plate mash first
  2. Place steak on plate and add a green garnish
  3. Enjoy!
