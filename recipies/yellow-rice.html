Yellow Rice

Description

Recipie for yellow rice. Nutritious side meal to be eaten with a protein of choice

Ingredients

  • 1/2 an onion
  • 1/2 a red bell pepper
  • Basmati rice
  • Tumeric
  • Dried lime
  • Cardamom seeds
  • Bay leaves
  • Chicken stock

Steps

Rice

  1. Dice onion and bell pepper
  2. Place olive oil in pot and heat on medium-high
  3. Put onion and bell pepper in after couple minutes of pot heating
  4. Fill kettle and boil
  5. Stir pot for a few minutes until soft and add salt & pepper
  6. Sieve basmati rice and rinse while intermittently stirring pot
  7. Add a few cardamom seeds, 2 dried limes and 2 bay leaves to pot, stirring
  8. Add teaspoon of tumeric to pot while stirring
  9. Add rice to pot and toast for a few minutes
  10. Dissolve a couple of chicken stock cubes in the boiling water in a bowl
  11. Add stock to rice and turn heat to max while stirring
  12. Once water has dried up, turn off heat

Serving

  • Serve with a main protein, chicken thighs are recommended
  • Optionally, top with a yoghurt sauce
  • Enjoy!
